# 🌍 Life Quest

Life Quest is a web application that turns everyday life into an adventure.

Instead of endless scrolling, users receive real-world side quests that encourage exploration, learning, creativity, fitness, personal growth, and meaningful experiences.

The goal is simple:

> Make life feel like a game worth playing.

---

## ✨ Features (Planned)

### Quest Generator
Generate random side quests based on different categories:

- Adventure
- Learning
- Creativity
- Fitness
- Spirituality
- Community
- Career
- Finance

### Difficulty Levels

- Common
- Rare
- Epic
- Legendary

### Character Progression

Complete quests to earn experience points (XP).

Potential stats include:

- Strength
- Intelligence
- Creativity
- Wisdom
- Charisma
- Wealth

### Adventure Log

Track completed quests and build a personal collection of memories and achievements.

---

## 🎯 Project Goals

Life Quest aims to help users:

- Spend less time scrolling
- Try new experiences
- Build useful skills
- Explore their local area
- Create memorable stories
- Improve personal growth through action

## 💡 Example Quests

- Complete a 30-day challenge
- Volunteer for a cause you care about

## 🌟 Vision

Life Quest is designed to encourage curiosity, growth, and adventure.

The long-term vision is to create a platform where people can collect experiences, level up real-life skills, and build a life filled with meaningful stories.

Every completed quest is a reminder that progress isn't just measured by productivity—it's measured by experiences.
